# Scribblesplash: The Borderless World

## Project Vision
To transform scribblesplash.com from a personal blog into a global anthropological company and humanitarian platform focused on the concept of "The Borderless World."

## Core Pillars
1. **Culture:** Celebrating nationality and diversity as a "cultural melting pot."
2. **Psychology:** Exploring the human mind to foster internal change towards unity.
3. **Awareness:** Contextualizing current events within the humanitarian mission.
4. **Community:** A platform for diverse storytelling (stories, articles, poems).

## Technical Conventions
- **AI-First:** Utilize AI for translation, content moderation, and personalized experiences.
- **Modern Stack:** Move towards a robust, scalable architecture (to be defined).
- **Transparency:** Clear documentation of business model and social impact.

## Workflow
- **Daily Progress:** Document all strategic and technical decisions in `MEMORY.md`.
- **Surgical Updates:** Use focused, verified changes to the codebase.
- **Validation:** Every feature must be verified against the core mission.
