Funny how they go buzzzz and wake you up in the middle of the night, you dear reader must've come across a mosquito not long ago! 
After long days work,  when the head hits the pillow you pay no heed to those things you planned to do when you get back home.  The cozy blankets inviting you in their bosom,  yeah that's the word isn't it? Bosom!  Haha no Better place to rest huh? Snap back [Pervy saint](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jiraiya_(Naruto))! So the brain finally starts to get the first dose of [melatonin](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Melatonin) it drifts away in the arms of sleep!   

And just as you appreciate the warmth and the snuggle you hear it like the londoners heard the [Luftwaffe](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Blitz) just before the blitz. The dreaded buzz!  

It's the only archnemesis remaining in this planet that we call home,  the mosquito! 

Imagine being an organism built by nature for one specific purpose!  Buzzkill!  Tu make sure you leave your comfort zone and get out to device an efficient way of just making them go away! 

Wait why are you doubting my motive here? This post is legit and will make sense soon,   I do have a point to make,  patience!

  So yeah we were imagining being a blood sucking mosquito! With a purpose,  and no they just need blood and have no clue what shitload of viruses,  bacteria, immigrants and hippopotamuses they transfer! But you see their whole body is somehow built in such a way that they can come in undetected, [drink up](https://g.co/kgs/oZAB6G) and leave,  I aint gonna go national geographic on your arse and talk about how their saliva has shit that thins out the blood and stuff, instead I want to say that nature is funny and marvelous at the same damn time! 

If you evolve to a specific purpose,  not only you get perks of transferring diseases that won't affect you but instead you are immune to them yourself, I never a saw any of those little buggers die of dengue!   you come in undetected do your thing and leave your mark,  what a frigging con artist! 

If we could simply learn to stop boasting about our qualities, deliver what we do best and leave and let our actions speak louder than our words, that leaves such an impact!  

Once you get the attention of your hosts,  you will be the only thing on their mind,  you will be in no them out of their comfort zone and you will make them notice you and put efforts into taking an action related to you!  

Smack!  We kill them right?  Well they're just insects but we hoooomans can learn, and evolve. 

Get up change your attitude change your thoughts,  evolve to be a certain someone with a purpose and just get going. 
The Waterman!
