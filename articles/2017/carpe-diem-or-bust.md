Imagine waking up so early in the morning,  wandering why the hell did u even turn on the other side that made you open your eyes and you reach out automagically to your phone only to see the alarm is not even close,  you realize you're swearing at your own sorry self for ruining valuable sleep well time. 

Well how about this what we just went through, doesn't define what  kinda day we're going to have ahead.

Imagine you left home early n did some things you never ever did in your sorry excuse for a life and you return home the next day and you say to yourself,  huh damn!  That wasn't so bad,  I can live another day. 

That's what I think life is,  full of uncertainty and full of turns. When you finally have that one day when you feel "yes I deserve it" then I'm sure it's been a good one.  Everyone is the same in this way they think they only deserve to be blessed with goodies. 

But somehow that one uncertain day which makes your life a little more livable is a result of a lot many days when you have cursed life for being too harsh on yourself. 

Wake up live on and sleep,  well how different are we from animals?  To be honest I haven't met a beaver with high blood pressure or a cat with diabetes so far...  I genuinely think that's because they do not live in the fear of things that might not even happen and moreover they don't live with a heart full of memories and a mind full of crap that is unnecessary. 

Tommorow when you wake up Just make sure you don't want to know what lies ahead of you.
