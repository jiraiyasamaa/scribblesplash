Did DC moviemakers just figure out the formula needed to make their movies as good as their animation movies? Or Is it a flash in the pan like gal Gadot's Wonder Woman. Well, it was Patty Jenkins' Wonder woman but y'all know what I mean.

Also hey Mister Terrific!! I loved his Darwin character in X-men First class n it was a pity to see him die after like 15 seconds on screen but hey he stood out! What a cool cucumber that was to watch and that fight sequence on the beach? I paid to go see Superman but hey I got a bonus!
speaking of bonuses. My boy Nathan was a showstopper! Seeing his holier than thou green lantern literally "flippin" tanks while "flippin" them is my teenage dream come true and how can one not forget his fellow serenity crew was voicing the robot number 4 was the pilot! I love how Castle makes sure his gangs' around him somehow!
Oh so many good things to say about this movie! Catered to all age groups I would say, childish humour made me chuckle. Nefarious villains made me pissed. Mindless villainy sidekicks that showed compassion yet had no hesitation hurting a puppy! Brilliance! So many subtle things I noticed like the crew handling Luthor's cockpit (ha-ha "pit") was cheering maniacally at each successful punch landed on red underpants! It somehow for me depicted how easily one's faith can sway basis what one is made to believe. Frenchie Boi

Jean-Jacques Rousseau said "Man was born free, and everywhere he is in chains" that’s what I remembered when I saw that sequence. Such a subtle hint towards ongoing geopolitics while making it evident that its literally happening around you as we speak.

What I loved about DC comics is they were different from Marvel. I give you that marvel Heros were never as OP as DC Heros usually are in the movies but that’s where DC was always getting them movies wrong!  Superman in the comics gets his ass handed to him on multiple times! And none of them did what this movie did today. It gave superman boxers!
Also, the dog did what a dog does! And come to think of it even though the dog was not a real dog, it still did what a dog does! It was hilarious to see that it did what it did thanks to bad pet parenting done by Rhaenyra Targaryen! No shit!
even the Mid credit and post credit scenes were fun! Anyway, it's time to go save some money cuz I think I'm going to get my hopes up for Fantastic 4 reboot number 5948 and hope Pedro "daddy" Pascal (GRR...) does what Nolan did to batman after the 1997 shitshow.
anyway! As a DC fanboy, kudos to every name in the credits (except for those who are assholes) (believe me some of them could be) for finally figuring their audiences out!
